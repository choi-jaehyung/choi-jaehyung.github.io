Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our data and mission.

How does my signature help?

Your signature is compiled into our monthly impact reports which are presented to industry policymakers and major platform executives to advocate for better creator protections.

Is my personal data safe?

We take privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and used solely for the purpose of verifying campaign numbers. We never sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

Why is illegal site usage so harmful?

Illegal sites drain the primary revenue source that platforms use to pay creators. Without this income, artists cannot afford basic costs, leading to serial cancellations.
